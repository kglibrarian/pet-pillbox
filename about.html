About Pet PillBox

An app for pet owners to help keep track of medication lists, costs, adverse events, contraindications for their pets. The app allows users to create, save, edit, or email themselves a medication list for each of their pets. They can use this list for doggy daycares, vet visits, and other places that might require a medication list for their pet.
